Waste overflow?

Amsterdam faces increasing trash pollution, with overflowing bins and improper waste disposal negatively impacting urban livability and waste management.

Vision!

NeatStreets is an innovative APP aiming to revolutionize urban waste management in Amsterdam. By enabling real-time tracking and fostering community collaboration, it helps create cleaner, more sustainable cities. Scalable and adaptable, NeatStreets empowers municipalities worldwide to promote greener urban environments.
